The Bleary-Eyed Bulletin · reporting from the Wreckage · Daily

Tuesday 7 July, and the Round of 16 signed off with heartbreak and heroics. Argentina saw off Egypt 3–2 — Messi a goal and an assist — while Switzerland and Colombia could not be parted across 120 goalless minutes, the Swiss taking the shootout 4–3 as Gregor Kobel saved the decisive kick. Egypt and Colombia are out. The night's story, though, was Tom's: a magnificent 33 points from his Colombia legion's clean sheet — and then the shootout tore the whole squad away. Jeremy's 28 lifts him to third; Joe A's Colombian defenders padded the lead to 351. The quarter-finals are set.
