The Bleary-Eyed Bulletin · reporting from the Wreckage · Daily
Sunday 21 June 2026 Matchday · Sat 20 Jun All games settled

Saturday 20 June was a feast of football and fantasy carnage in equal measure. Netherlands 5–1 Sweden was the day's showpiece, Cody Gakpo and Crysencio Summerville running riot and making certain managers very happy indeed. Germany 2–1 Côte d'Ivoire gave us Deniz Undav's brace and a clutch of modest returns. Tunisia 0–4 Japan was a demolition job — Ayase Ueda bagging a brace and Daichi Kamada adding to the misery of anyone who backed the Tunisians. And Ecuador 0–0 Curaçao was exactly as thrilling as it sounds, though Pervis Estupiñán quietly pocketed 9 points for the patient among us. Four games, plenty of goals (well, in three of them), and a standings shake-up that'll have at least one manager spitting out their morning coffee.
