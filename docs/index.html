The Bleary-Eyed Bulletin · reporting from the Wreckage · Daily
Sunday 21 June 2026 Matchday · Sat 20 Jun All games settled

Saturday delivered four crackers: Netherlands 5–1 Sweden, Germany 2–1 Côte d'Ivoire, Ecuador 0–0 Curaçao, and Tunisia 0–4 Japan. The Dutch put on a show, Gakpo and Summerville running riot, while Japan's Ueda bagged a brace to humiliate Tunisia. Germany edged a feisty one against the Ivorians, Deniz Undav netting twice. Ecuador and Curaçao cancelled each other out in the dullest goalless draw of the tournament so far. Fantasy points flew around like confetti in Amsterdam and Osaka — and barely stirred in Quito.
