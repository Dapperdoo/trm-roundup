Wednesday 24 June 2026 Group Matchday 2 · Tuesday 23 June Matchday settled

Round Two signed off with a bang on Tuesday, and one man cleaned up. Portugal ran riot 5-0 over Uzbekistan to gift Malik's all-Portugal armada an absurd 56-point haul, while a goalless England–Ghana stalemate quietly rained clean-sheet points across half the league. Croatia edged Panama 1-0 and Colombia saw off DR Congo 1-0, where Daniel Muñoz's strike helped fire Tom to the summit. As the dust settles on the group's second round, it's Tomo on top — but Malik's Lisbon fireworks were the story of the night.

1stAnamaduwa Athletic · Tom164 pts

Tomo Tops the Pile from a Bar Stool in Asia

Forty-two points and somehow top of the entire tree — Tom probably hasn't even noticed, lost between time zones with a curry in hand. Daniel Muñoz (14) struck in Colombia's 1-0 win over DR Congo, Ezri Konsa (9) and Jordan Pickford (7) banked the clean-sheet points from England's goalless draw, and a Colombian chorus of Jhon Arias (4), Luis Díaz (3) and Luis Suárez (1) chipped in, with Pedro Neto (4) adding a Portuguese flourish. From perennial foot-of-the-table scrapper to 164 and the summit — and rival Nick is a full 41 points back and surely bellowing about it. Don't tell Tomo; he thinks he's watching the cricket.

Propaganda's Portuguese Armada Detonates for 56

Fifty-six. In a single night.
