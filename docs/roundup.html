The Bleary-Eyed Bulletin · reporting from the Wreckage · Daily

Sunday 5 July, and two of the giants fell. Norway stunned Brazil 2–1 — Erling Haaland with both goals — and England saw off Mexico 3–2 behind a Jude Bellingham brace and a Harry Kane penalty. Brazil and Mexico are out, and a swathe of owned stars go with them. Wigs and Jake shared the night on 19 apiece: Bellingham's 15 the standout, and Jake's long-dormant Harry Kane finally repaying a sliver of that outlay. At the summit, Joe A's quiet four was enough to keep Shatner's Bassoon top; Sam's Haaland heroics keep him within seven.
