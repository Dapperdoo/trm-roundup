The Bleary-Eyed Bulletin · reporting from the Wreckage · Daily

Thursday 2 July belonged to the old guard. Spain dismantled Austria 3–0, Portugal edged Croatia 2–1 with Ronaldo among the scorers, and Switzerland saw off Algeria 2–0 — Austria, Croatia and Algeria all knocked out. But the night's real headline wasn't written by any algorithm: it was Jeremy, the traditionalist who wouldn't let a line of code near his team sheet, plundering a tournament-best 36 points to erupt from twelfth to fifth. At the summit, Sam blanked entirely and didn't so much as blink — 286 points, and a lead you could park a bus in.
